Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Welcome to the privacy policy of WearSSH. This application connects to SSH servers directly from your Wear OS device — no intermediary servers, cloud services, or third-party infrastructure are involved.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

WearSSH does not collect, store, or share any personal data whatsoever. All SSH connection details (host, port, username, keys) remain exclusively on your device.

2. No Server Infrastructure

WearSSH operates entirely client-side. There are no backend servers, no analytics, no crash reporting services, and no data relay — all network traffic is direct SSH protocol communication from your watch to the server you specify.

3. SSH Credentials

Any SSH private keys, passwords, or host configurations you enter are stored locally on your Wear OS device. They are never transmitted anywhere except to the SSH server you explicitly connect to.

4. Third-Party Services

WearSSH does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect, monitor, or analyze user data.

5. Permissions

The app requires internet permission solely for establishing SSH connections. No other device permissions are requested or used for data collection.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
